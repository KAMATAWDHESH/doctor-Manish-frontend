Meet Dr. Manish Ranjan

Dr. Manish Ranjan is an orthopedic surgeon with over 17 years of experience. With more than 5,000 orthopedic procedures successfully completed, he has earned a reputation for providing his patients with the best possible care.

A wide variety of specialties are available to Dr. Ranjan, including arthroscopy, periacetabular surgery, hand surgery, bone tumors, foot and ankle surgery, spine surgery, and complicated trauma. A specialist in orthopedics with extensive training and experience, he is committed to providing the highest level of care to each of his patients.

Choosing the best orthopedic surgeon in Patna can be a challenging process. If you are looking for an experienced and highly skilled orthopedic doctor, Dr. Manish Ranjan is the person you should contact. His expertise in orthopedics has helped numerous patients achieve better mobility and quality of life.

Educational Excellence

Dr. Ranjan's journey to becoming the best orthopedic surgeon in Patna is marked by a relentless pursuit of knowledge and expertise.

He completed his MBBS from Patna Medical College and Hospital (PMCH) and went on to pursue his MS in Orthopedics from the prestigious All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi.

He has also received advanced training in arthroscopy and joint replacement surgery from renowned institutes in India and abroad, equipping him with a deep understanding of orthopedic conditions and their treatments.

He regularly attends national and international conferences and workshops to learn about the latest techniques and technologies in the field. This enables him to provide his patients with the most advanced and effective treatments available.

Patient-Centered Care
